About Me

I am a computer scientist.

The purpose is to create an artifical intellegence to play Capture the Flag. The long-term goal is to get teenagers interested in learning to program with Python.

As a teaching assistant for Operating Systems, I am excited to work on a Linux-based Android operating system. The projects comming out of this semester will be very exciting.

My independent study this semseter is all things cyber security. I will participate in Capture the Flag hacking competitions and attend cyber secruity networking events. You can follow my journey into cyber security through my blog.
